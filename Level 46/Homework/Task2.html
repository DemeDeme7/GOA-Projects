Simple & Delicious Recipe

This is an easy and delicious recipe that you can make in no time.

Preparation Time

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 30 minutes
  • Total: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour and salt together.
  2. Add the water and oil.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  4. Bake at 180Â°C for 30 minutes.
