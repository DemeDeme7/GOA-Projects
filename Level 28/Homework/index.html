My Favourite Animals:

  1. Dogs

    Dogs are from an extinct population of wolves,being the first species to be "accepted" by humans.There are over 300 dog breeds as of now.
  2. Wolves

    Wolves are strong animals and the species that "created" dogs.
  3. Cats

    The cat (Felis catus), also referred to as the domestic cat or house cat, is a small domesticated carnivorous mammal.
  4. Rabbits

    Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae (which also includes the hares), which is in the order Lagomorpha (which also includes pikas).
  5. Hamsters

    Hamsters are rodents (order Rodentia) belonging to the subfamily Cricetinae, which contains 19 species classified in seven genera.
