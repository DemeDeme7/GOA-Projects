Top 5 favourite foods:

  1. Khinkali

    Khinkali is a dumpling-style food which originates from Georgia.It is made with twisted knobs of dough at the top,and it's usually
    filled with meat.

  2. Shawarma

    Shawarma is a middle-eastern dish which was originated from the Ottoman Empire,which includes meat that is cut in thin slices.
    In Georgia,Shawarma is a food which is loved by many people,and the most famous type is the Pork Shawarma wrapped in Lavash.

  3. Adjaruli Khachapuri

    Adjaruli Khachapuri is a traditional Georgian dish which is loved by many people.It's a cheese bread,with an optional egg
    in the middle.

  4. Burgers

    Burgers(Hamburgers) Originaly come from Hamburg,Germany.It's made up with lettuce,a burger patty,sometimes tomatos,ketchup,sauces,etc.

  5. Fried Chicken

    Fried chicken, also called Southern fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried.
